## Key Differences

### AI Agent Integration
Tech Scouting Report MCP is built for AI agents using the MCP protocol. AI agents call `scout_technology(company="AI startup")` and get a full intelligence report: research momentum, patent landscape, funding signals, TRL assessment, and investment verdict.

PitchBook and Crunchbase are investor-facing platforms with REST APIs designed for enterprise integration with high costs and access restrictions.

### Unique Data: Research Momentum + TRL
Tech Scouting Report MCP uniquely combines OpenAlex/arXiv research momentum scoring, NIH funding signals, and Technology Readiness Level (TRL) assessment — combining academic and commercial data sources.

### Pricing Model
At $0.05/call, you get 2,000 technology scouting reports for the price of one PitchBook month — without an enterprise contract.

## When to Choose Each

**Choose Tech Scouting Report MCP when:**
- You're an AI agent doing VC or corporate R&D scouting
- You need research momentum + patent + funding in one call
- You want TRL assessment for technology evaluation
- You need pay-per-use without enterprise contracts
- You're building an investment intelligence product

**Choose PitchBook when:**
- Your team does manual VC/M&A research
- You have budget for premium investor data
- You need M&A deal flow data alongside scouting

**Choose Crunchbase when:**
- Your team does startup research
- You have budget for startup intelligence
- You need funding data alongside company info
